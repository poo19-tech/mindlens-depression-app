How it works

Two models, one pipeline.

  1. Face detection — OpenCV's Haar Cascade locates the face in the frame.
  2. Classification — A fine-tuned MobileNetV2 network, trained on a balanced set of depressed / non-depressed labeled faces, scores the crop.
  3. Decision — Above 50% confidence toward "depressed," the badge reads Depressed; otherwise, Not Depressed.
